STICKS AND STONES

Player X'S turn

Instructions


This classic game never gets old, to start simply click on any square,
its a two player game so make sure your opponent is ready for a challenge.
You can either be team X or O.
The aim is to align three of your icons either X or O, in a row,
thats either up, down, left, right and even diagonally.. Challenge a friend and let the games begin..
